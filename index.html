Introducing... the Duck Desktop Buddy!

Have you ever felt like Natalie's website was... a little lonely? You would no doubt be spending hours here if it weren't for that fact. Alternatively, you just want to learn more about what I could put under "ducks" and why I wanted them on this site.

Well the answer to that is here! Nice! What those of you who have seen me coding - or talked to me about coding extensions - may already be aware of, I am an avid VS-Code Pets fan! So the desktop duck buddy (or ducktop buddy ba-dum-tssh) is a little nod to my setup. It was also partially inspired by the game Placid Plastic Duck Simulator!

I also added biography content and the name backstories for each of the ducks.

So how do you use this? You can select any of the ducks below and hit launch, which will then spawn the chosen duck in the bottom right-hand corner of the site. To keep it running while you look at the rest of the content, please select the minimize button. Hitting close will exit the program entirely. Happy duck watching!

Higgs Boson

Birthday: 10/27/20

A clubmate from the sci-fi club was doing a (distanced!) Halloween event, passing out ducks and candy to any members that wanted them. Being very physics focused at the time, I named him after the Higgs Boson particle. For the rabbit-hole oriented among you, I recommend the related topic 'sombrero potential'.

Napstar

Birthday: 3/24/23

A professor in the department had a surplus of rubber ducks he was giving out to students. Known for my love of pirates, this duck was grabbed and given to me. The name comes from the classic music piracy software of the same name. Why "Napstar" in particular? Its logo had a similar purple color to the vest!

Like Tommy, was a gift from one of my brothers. Named after one of the Nooklings in the animal crossing series, this name comes namely from the duck being part of a duo, and one that references gaming.

Nami

Birthday: 10/18/23

ACMW - mentorship duck decorating event. I saw the headphones and knew I had to incorperate them somehow. Did not really plan anything, but went for a "gaming accessory" aesthetic. Nami is a character that I use for a gaming pfp. The keyboard featured on the baseplate uses the QWERTY layout.

Nimda

Birthday: 10/29/23

tOSU's 2023 hackathon gave out many forms of swag. My personal favorite was obviously, the duck. Due to the numerous hacking related jokes my groupmates and I made, I wanted to give this duck a name related to something compsci/hacking/virus related. So, this duck is named after the nimda virus* - a malicious file infecting worm - very fun! :)
*which is 'admin' spelled backwords!
